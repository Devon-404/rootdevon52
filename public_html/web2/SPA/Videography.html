Shot Lists

What is a Shot List?

Organizing your shots to make your video more structured and professional.

A shot list is your game plan for filming—it keeps you organized, saves time, and makes sure you don’t forget any important shots. Instead of scrambling on site, you’ll know exactly what angles, movements, and frames you need to bring your vision to life. Whether you're making a short film, a vlog, or just experimenting, a solid shot list helps everything run smoother and makes your final video look way more polished.
